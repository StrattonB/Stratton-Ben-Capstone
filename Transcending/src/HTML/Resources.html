resources We Offer!

Transcending offers assistance to the LGBTQIA+ community with medical (mental and physical), legal, and spiritual wellness. All programs are subject to funding availability. These programs include:


  • Visits with a doctor
  • Visits with a therapist
  • Visits with a lawyer
  • Support groups


New programs will always be discussed and added so feel free to check back in the future!

In addition to the resources we provide, Transcending also offers educational events and programs for members of the public to learn more about the LGBTQIA+ community.

Please follow us on Facebook for updates on events as we have them!
