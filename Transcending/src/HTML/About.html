About Transcending

Transcending is a non-profit organization (pending IRS 501(c)(3) status). Our mission is to assist members of the LGBTQIA+ community live safely and happily by providing support and resources to members of the community as well as education to allies and members of the general public. Our vision is to unite the LGBTQIA+ community so that we may support each other and to erase the stigma surrounding the community.

About The Board of Directors

As a non-profit organization that is focused on meeting the needs of the LGBTQIA+ community, the Board of Directors aims to be friendly, approachable, and helpful. Every person who serves on the Board of Directors is dedicated to our mission and they strive to make our vision come true.
